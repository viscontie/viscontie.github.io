CampusCare

Issue to Address

Mental health and wellness resources at Carleton are not easily accessible and often fragmented.

  • Resources are spread across multiple platforms
  • Misinformation and lack of information prevalent among student body
  • Lack of navigable centralized information hub
  • Resources and important information about accessing them can get lost in current campus websites

Our Goal

Ensure every Carleton student has easy access to all wellness resources within our campus and local community.

Low Fidelity Prototype

Our app design addresses these issues by connecting Carleton students with different places where they can find mental health and well-being resources. The homepage of our app will feature well-being events of the day, quick links to commonly utilized resources like scheduling counseling appointments, and access to online healthcare.

The app will include:

  • Resources Page: Laying out all mental health-related organizations and resources available to Carleton students and where to access them.
  • Fast FAQs: Aiming to combat misinformation and lack of information by answering commonly asked questions like transportation to appointments off-campus, how to make counseling appointments, or use school insurance.
  • Wellness Calendar: Allows users to select upcoming dates and see events related to mental health and well-being happening that day.
